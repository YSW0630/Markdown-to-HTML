# What to Eat in Paris

## 1. [La Terrasse du 7e](https://maps.app.goo.gl/K6i378RUjKZW68758)

⭐ 4.1

**Location:**  
*2 Pl. de l'École Militaire, 75007 Paris, France*

![La Terrasse du 7e](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/b9f1b6_d78ab38ea4fd4ddabd149e7060a2800e~mv2.jpg/v1/crop/x_0,y_83,w_1542,h_2146/fill/w_447,h_622,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/terrasse-1.jpg "Outdoor seating available")

**Highlights:**

+ Outdoor tables
+ Large escargots
+ Thick baked onion soup
+ Smoked salmon with bread

Note: Might be too salty

## 3. [Astrance](https://maps.app.goo.gl/rCGaRqtQDteVPNxZ9)

⭐ 4.5

**Location:**  
*32 Rue de Longchamp, 75116 Paris, France*

![Astrance](https://www.elizabethonfood.com/files/Liesbeth%20Auerbach/image/astrance/astranceinterior.jpg)

**Highlights:**

+ Known as the most affordable Michelin-starred restaurant in Paris (lunch menu)
+ Everyone must order the same set menu
+ Fusion of Asian (Chinese/Japanese) and French cuisine

⭐ 4.0

**Location:**  
*1 Pl. du Trocadéro et du 11 Novembre, 75016 Paris, France*

![Girafe Restaurant](https://magazineluxe.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/restaurant-girafe-paris-haute-gastronomie-high-gastronomy-luxe-magazine-2-1300x867.jpg)
